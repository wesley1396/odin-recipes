THE BEST CEREAL ALWAYS DELICIOUS REESE'S PUFFS

Whether it's summer or winter, you could always count on Reeses puffs to deliver a 1-2 punch of great flavor and full stomach!

Follow this recipe to make the perfect bowl! Even beginners cant mess this up.

You will need:

  • 1 Bowl (prefereably large)
  • 1 Spoon (the bigger the better)
  • Milk of your choice
  • Box of Reese's Puffs cereal

Here's how to make it

  • Pour desired amount of cereal in bowl.
  • Place spoon in bowl.
  • Pour desired amount of cold whole milk into bowl.
  • ENJOY!
