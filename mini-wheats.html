Marvelous Mini-wheats

Follow this recipe closely, and you too could enjoy this top rated cereal! Be sure to follow steps IN ORDER!

  1. Grab 1 bowl, 1 box of mini wheats, 1 spoon, and whole milk
  2. Pour desired amount of cereal in bowl.
  3. Place spoon in bowl.
  4. Pour desired amount of cold whole milk into bowl.
  5. ENJOY!
